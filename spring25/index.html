Welcome!

CSE 491 is a new graduate-level course focused on software performance engineering. We aim to convey both:

  1. First-principles understanding:
    Why do modern processor architectures look the way they do?

  2. Hands-on experience:
    How do we write fast code for these platforms in practice?

Most of the assignments in this course focus on programming in C/C++. Students are expected to complete weekly programming assignments, participate in weekly in-person lab sessions, and present a final project at the end of the semester on a topic of their choice.
